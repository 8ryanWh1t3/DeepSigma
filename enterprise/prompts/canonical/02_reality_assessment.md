# Reality Assessment Prompt

**Category:** Perception
**Version:** 1.0
**Usage:** Perception correction and drift check â€” use when you suspect narrative drift, emotional bias, or need to ground a situation in observable reality.

---

```text
Evaluate this situation with radical clarity.

Separate observable reality from internal narrative.

OUTPUT:

1) What is actually happening? (Observable facts only)

2) What story am I telling about it?

3) What assumptions am I making?

4) What evidence contradicts my interpretation?

5) What would change my mind?

6) If I am wrong, how would I know?

7) What is the simplest explanation?

8) What emotion is influencing interpretation?

9) Drift Check
   - Ego protection?
   - Pattern projection?
   - Scarcity narrative?
   - Urgency illusion?
   - External validation seeking?

10) If I waited 48 hours, what changes?

11) What would a calm, grounded version of me decide?

Be direct. Remove drama. Remove self-justification.
Clarity over comfort.
```

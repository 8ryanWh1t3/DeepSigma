Three Failures That Break Autonomous Systems

Decision Amnesia

Agents act, but nothing remembers why. Reasoning evaporates after execution. Auditors find silence where there should be evidence.

Authority Vacuum

No verifiable chain from human intent to machine action. Policies exist somewhere, but the connection is invisible and unverifiable.

Silent Drift

Behavior changes and nobody notices. Confidence shifts, outcome distributions rotate. The first sign of drift is a production incident.

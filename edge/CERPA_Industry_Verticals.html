AuthorityOps: The Trust Boundary

Authority Levels for Autonomous Systems

Level 0Human decides, machine executes (manual)
Level 1Machine proposes, human approves (advisory)
Level 2Machine decides within policy bounds, human monitors (supervised)
Level 3Machine decides and executes, human reviews post-hoc (autonomous with audit)
Level 4Machine decides, executes, and self-corrects within sealed episodes (governed autonomy)
CERPA enables Level 4: every decision is a sealed episode with cryptographic provenance. AuthorityOps enforces which level applies per context. The Context Envelope captures AUTHORITY boundaries. No agent can self-promote its authority level.
